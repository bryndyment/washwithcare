Wash With Care is a community-based, collaborative research project aimed at raising awareness about pesticide safety in BC's farming community. The project is multi-disciplinary in nature and includes farm community members, local community groups as well as environmental scientists, policy makers and communications experts from UBC, SFU, and UCFV.
